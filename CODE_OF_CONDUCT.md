# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want Cahootz to be a welcoming, constructive project for people building community-owned economies. We expect everyone who participates to act with respect, care, and good faith.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful of different backgrounds, skill levels, and lived experiences.
- Give and receive feedback constructively.
- Keep discussions focused on the work and the people affected by it.
- Credit ideas, code, documentation, and community labor.
- Assume good intent, but take responsibility for impact.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, intimidation, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks, trolling, sustained disruption, or bad-faith arguments.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Sexualized language or imagery in project spaces.
- Any behavior that undermines a safe and collaborative community.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or restrict participation when behavior violates this code of conduct.

To report a concern, email `team@cahootz.coop`. Reports will be handled with care and shared only with people who need to help resolve the issue.
